Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions by our users:

  • How do I create an account?
    To create an account, click on the signup button on the home page and fill in the required details.
  • How can I reset my password?
    If you forget your password, click on 'Forgot Password' on the login page to receive a reset link.
  • What should I do if I encounter a bug?
    Please contact our support team with a description of the issue you are facing.
  • Can I change my profile information?
    Yes, you can edit your profile information from the account settings page.
  • Is there a mobile app available?
    Yes, our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices.
